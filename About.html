Sustainable skin care

GRIND is a hand-made body scrub with all natural ingredients. In order to support sustainability, our coffee grounds are collected from local cafes in Bath. With our product, we are also seeking to raise awareness on self care and sexual health issues with funny, catchy quotes and advice on the lids of our jars.

Carefully prepared by the members of Enactus Bath and tested in the Biology department of the University of Bath, GRIND consists of 3 main ingredients.

Coffee grounds

Collected from local cafes with a purpose to support sustainability, it exfoliates the skin, energizes and combats acne.
